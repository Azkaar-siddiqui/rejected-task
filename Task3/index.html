Muzaffarpur - "Land of Shahi Litchi"

Muzaffarpur is famous for its Shahi Litchi, known for its unique sweetness, aroma, and juicy texture.

The region's soil and climate create ideal conditions for litchi cultivation, making it one of the largest producers in India.

Muzaffarpur's litchis have received a Geographical Indication (GI) tag, highlighting their authenticity and quality.

Every year, the fruit attracts traders and exporters, boosting the local economy.

Baba Garibnath Temple is one of Muzaffarpur's most revered landmarks, attracting thousands of devotees throughout the year.

Jubba Sahni Park is a peaceful retreat for families and tourists, offering lush greenery and space for relaxation.
